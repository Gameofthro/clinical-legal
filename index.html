II. Authentication & User Identity

ClinicalAssist uses Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication) to verify user identity. This is required to personalize your clinical workspace and protect your professional profile.

When you sign in with Google, the following occurs:

  • GOOGLE ACCOUNT: Your Google account credentials are managed exclusively by Google LLC. ClinicalAssist never receives, stores, or has access to your Google password.
  • FIREBASE AUTHENTICATION: We use Firebase Authentication (by Google) solely to verify your identity. Firebase receives your Google account identifier (UID) and email address for this purpose.
  • LOCAL PROFILE STORAGE: After sign-in, your chosen display name and professional designation (Doctor or Student) are stored exclusively in encrypted local storage on your physical device using SQLite. This data is never transmitted to ClinicalAssist servers.
  • NO PHI INPUT: You are strictly prohibited from entering any patient-identifiable information including patient names, medical record numbers, diagnoses, or biometric data into any field in this application.
  • TERMS ACCEPTANCE: Your agreement to these Terms of Service is recorded locally on your device. A new agreement is required if these terms are materially updated.

Firebase Authentication is governed by Google's Privacy Policy available at policies.google.com/privacy. By using ClinicalAssist you also agree to Google's Terms of Service.

III. AI-Assisted Clinical Support — The Assistant

Important Notice About AI Features

The Assistant is an AI-powered clinical decision support feature available to ClinicalAssist Pro subscribers. All AI responses are grounded in verified data from official medical registries. AI responses are for reference only and must be verified before clinical application.

The Assistant feature operates as follows:

  • DATA SOURCES: The Assistant grounds its responses exclusively in verified data from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Drug Label Database, FDA MedWatch Post-Marketing Surveillance System, and the NIH National Library of Medicine Clinical Tables API. No speculative or unverified information is intentionally generated.
  • AI ENGINE: Query synthesis is performed using a large language model. Queries are transmitted securely over HTTPS to the AI provider's API endpoint for processing. Queries are not stored or used to train AI models under standard API usage terms.
  • QUERY CONTENT: Clinical queries you submit to The Assistant are transmitted to the AI processing endpoint. You must never include patient-identifiable information in any query.
  • NO QUERY LOGGING BY CLINICALASSIST: ClinicalAssist does not log, store, or review the content of your queries. Query processing is handled entirely by the AI provider under their data processing terms.
  • UNCERTAINTY DISCLOSURE: The Assistant is programmed to explicitly flag when data is insufficient or uncertain and to direct users to verify with current clinical guidelines.
  • PRO SUBSCRIPTION: The Assistant is a premium feature available via a paid subscription processed through Razorpay. Payment data is handled exclusively by Razorpay and is never accessible to ClinicalAssist.

IV. Data We Collect and Why

ClinicalAssist is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service. No data is sold to third parties. Ever.

The following data is processed when you use ClinicalAssist:

  • Google Account UID and Email: Collected via Firebase Authentication at sign-in. Used solely to verify identity and link your local profile. Stored by Firebase under Google's privacy policy.
  • Display Name and Designation: Entered by you during onboarding. Stored locally on your device in SQLite encrypted storage. Never transmitted to ClinicalAssist servers.
  • Premium Subscription Status: Your Pro subscription status is stored locally on your device. Payment verification is handled by Razorpay.
  • Clinical Queries (Pro users only): Queries submitted to The Assistant are transmitted to the AI processing endpoint over HTTPS. Not stored by ClinicalAssist.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging Token: If you grant notification permission, a device token is registered with Firebase to enable push notifications. This token does not identify you personally.
  • Crash and Performance Data: Anonymous crash reports may be collected by Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. No personal or clinical data is included.

We do not collect:

  • Patient data, medical records, or Protected Health Information (PHI) of any kind
  • Device location data
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data
  • Biometric data of any kind

V. Where Your Data Is Stored

  • LOCAL DEVICE STORAGE (SQLite): Your name, designation, and app preferences are stored in encrypted SQLite on your physical Android device. This data never leaves your device under normal operation.
  • FIREBASE (Google Cloud): Your Google UID and email are stored in Firebase Authentication infrastructure operated by Google in accordance with their data residency policies.
  • RAZORPAY: Payment and subscription data is stored by Razorpay in accordance with PCI-DSS standards and RBI guidelines for Indian payment processors.

VI. Account Deletion & Data Removal

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. ClinicalAssist provides multiple ways to exercise this right.

To delete your account and data:

  • IN-APP DELETION: Open the app → tap the menu icon (top right) → Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Secure Logout". This wipes your local SQLite profile, clears all locally stored preferences, and signs you out of Firebase Authentication on this device.
  • FULL DATA DELETION REQUEST: To request permanent deletion of your Firebase Authentication record (Google UID and email), contact us at clinicalassist.center@gmail.com with subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process this within 30 days and confirm via email.
  • UNINSTALL: Uninstalling the app from your Android device automatically purges all locally stored SQLite data, preferences, and cached content from your device.
  • RAZORPAY SUBSCRIPTION: To cancel your Pro subscription, contact Razorpay support or email us at clinicalassist.center@gmail.com. Cancellation stops future billing; access continues until the end of the current cycle.

In accordance with Google Play's Data Deletion Policy, users who created an account can request deletion of their account and associated data both within the app and via the contact email above.
